About Me

I am currently working at Samsung Research India Bangalore. I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Kharagpur where I was fortunate to work with Prof Pawan Goyal (bachelor's thesis guide), Prof Niloy Ganguly, and Prof Saptarshi Ghosh at Complex Networks Research Group (CNeRG) Lab.

Currently my work at Samsung is towards building AI driven healthcare solutions using multimodal sensor data from wearable devices. During my bachelor's I worked towards building generalizable LLM frameworks in the domain of Multi Label Text Classification and Financial Numerical Reasoning. As an avid user of LLM technology, I am motivated to advance its capabilities in numerical reasoning and multimodal question answering. Given the rapid scaling in this field, I am equally focused on exploring pretraining strategies and architectural refinements to develop truly interactive and generalizable systems.
