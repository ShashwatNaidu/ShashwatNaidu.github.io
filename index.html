About Me

I am currently working at Samsung Research India Bangalore. I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Kharagpur where I was fortunate to work with Prof Pawan Goyal (bachelor's thesis guide), Prof Niloy Ganguly and Prof Saptarshi Ghosh at CNeRG Lab.

Currently my work at Samsung is towards building AI driven healthcare solutions using multimodal sensor data from wearable devices. During my bachelor's I worked towards building generalizable LLM frameworks in the domain of Multi Label Text Classification and Financial Numerical Reasoning.
